MERCEDES LEON

Gepost op 22 mei 2016

My name is Mercedes Leon.
I'm an illustrator, cartoonist and graphic designer currently based in London.
I was born 26 years ago in Spain.
After living in Barcelona, I studied Fine Arts in Madrid (Universidad Complutense).
I started freelancing in 2010, working as graphic designer and art director for some studios and agencies in North Spain, before leaving my dear peninsula to live my London dream.
I've finished a MA in Visual Arts in 2012 at Camberwell College (UAL), and so far I have collaborated with Wellcome Collection, Craft Central and Peckham Space.
I'm currently working as freelance for Tigerprint (a division of Hallmark Cards, supplier of M&S) and Financial Times Publishing (illustrating a bussiness book due to be published in July).
